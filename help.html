FAQs

Q: How do I use the Panic Button?

A: Open Dashboard → Tap Panic → Alert sent (demo).

Q: How do I enable tracking?

A: Toggle the tracking button on the Tourist Dashboard (demo-only).

Troubleshooting
  • If geolocation is unavailable, ensure browser location permissions are enabled.
  • If demo alerts are not visible, open the Geo-Fence demo page and trigger an alert by moving the marker outside the fence.
